chicken-wings recipe

Description

Crispy oven Baked Chicken wings are so easy to prepare and you dont habe to bother with grease form frying

A healthier yet delicious way to enjoy wings ðŸ˜‹

Ingredients

  • 4 pounds of chicken wings
  • 2 tablespoons baking powder
  • 3/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon garlic

Steps

  1. Adjust your oven rack to the upper-middle position
  2. line a baking sheet with aluminum foil and place a wire rack
  3. Use paper towels to pat the wings dry and place them in a large bowl.
  4. Combine the salt, pepper, garlic powder, paprika , and baking powder in a small bowl
  5. arrange wings , skin up
  6. bake on the upper middle oven rack , turning every 20 mins
  7. Remove from oven and let stand for 5 minutes.
